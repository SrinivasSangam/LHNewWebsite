Apple Watch Application for a Healthcare company

A leading Healthcare company wanted LiquidHub to develop an Apple watch app that would not only be useful but would also be beneficial to the customer’s health.

Solution

LiquidHub delivered an innovative application that could provide account information, connect with providers, Build reminders for medication schedules and request refills from the pharmacy. A reporting and tracking system was developed that could record daily mood. Our solution encouraged stress reduction, and displayed side by side reports on activity, frame of mind, and steps.

Impact

LiquidHub’s solution was a success and it helped our clients to record, track, and monitor their health. The application was able to simplify daily activities, such as observing a good nights rest, timely reminders for medications, illustrating breathing exercises, and making an overall positive effect on daily lives.
