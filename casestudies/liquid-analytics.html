Liquid Analytics: Patient MDM 2.0 Implementation

A multinational biotechnology company had different departments and multiple communication channels that were independent to each other and being used to interact with patients. There was no provision to consolidate all the patient information which they received as a part of their interactions. They wanted to develop a master database which would help with consolidation and integration of all the information.

Solution

LiquidHub helped to deploy a platform that aided to ingest, cleanse, validate, match and merge patient demographic data. We enabled creation of a unique record for each patient. LiquidHub also assisted with building a cross reference link to the various source systems.

Impact

LiquidHub helped to bring a significant change in the way patient information is managed across the enterprise. The patients were supported with better access to treatment options. We facilitated the smooth management of compliance, patient services and marketing consents.
