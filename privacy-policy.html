Privacy Policy

LiquidHub, Inc. is firmly committed to your privacy. As a visitor, you should understand what we do with any information you provide to us while using our site, www.liquidhub.com. This Privacy Policy discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices.

Information Collection and Use

As a visitor, you do not have to provide any personal information in order to use our website. Personal information is not collected through our site unless it is specifically and voluntarily provided by visitors. Such information may consist of, but is not limited to, your name, email address, company name, current job title, and telephone number. As a general rule, sensitive information is not collected on our site.

Information Collection and Use

Using liquidhub.com does not require registration of any kind other than voluntary registration for downloading specified LiquidHub content, signing up for email and newsletter updates, and filling out a contact form. All personal data collected will not be sold to anyone, and will be solely used by LiquidHub for future contact with the registrants. Any information that is collected or stored about you may be transferred to a future purchaser of our company and/or its assets.

Changes to our Privacy Policy

LiquidHub, Inc. reserves the right to modify or update our Privacy Policy at any time without notice. We encourage you to review this policy from time to time.
