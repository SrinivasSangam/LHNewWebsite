DIGITAL ARCHITECTURE SERVICES:
Since our founding, LiquidHub has been passionate about Architecture. Our approach, proven templates, and industry expertise, combined with a structured governance and metrics model, deliver a uniquely adaptive IT framework. Guiding our architecture implementations is our business-tested Digital Architecture Toolkit, which consists of LiquidHub Frameworks, Methodologies, Meta-Models, Templates, and Roadmap (NEXAR™).

DIGITAL ARCHITECTURE PROGRAM INITIATION
Understanding how your organization currently operates is the first step toward effecting change. At LiquidHub, we work with our clients to strategize, design, and implement Digital Architecture that helps drive business initiatives. A critical component of organization and management, Digital Architecture maps out a company’s business processes within an IT environment and creates a blueprint that address your biggest IT challenges. Maybe your company is starting from the ground up with a brand-new EA system, or perhaps your current EA hasn’t kept pace with your company’s growth and is now obsolete. From a current-state assessment and target-state strategy to gap analysis, governance and road mapping, LiquidHub designs and tailors EA solutions to fit our clients’ unique needs.

DIGITAL ARCHITECTURE ADVISORY SERVICES
When functioning at optimum efficiency, a well-crafted EA ensures superior business performance. However, as your organization grows and changes, some or all of the existing EA components may need to be replaced, extended or optimized. At LiquidHub, we use our experience and expertise to identify and address our clients’ EA issues. We focus on governance and the four sub-architectures of EA: business, application, information, and technology, and measure them against performance metrics.

Our Digital Architecture blueprinting service manages the complex relationship between business processes, information, applications, and technology infrastructure as well as governance. It identifies the system components of an organization and defines how these components synchronize to achieve defined business goals.

SOLUTION ARCHITECTURE
Each solution has its unique architecture that integrates the various, divergent elements of EA – technology, information, application and business processes – to solve a business challenge. By focusing on how the various layers of digital architecture interact, Solution Architecture can ensure robustness, sustainability and the long-term business value of a solution. Solution Architecture also includes rules and actions to make solutions reusable, cost-effective, and easier to implement across the Enterprise.
